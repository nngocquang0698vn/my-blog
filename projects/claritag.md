Claritag is a <a href="https://clarifai.com">Clarifai</a> application that organises your Dropbox photos for you. The application is built in <a href="http://flask.pocoo.org/">Flask</a> and integrates with the <a href="https://www.dropbox.com/developers">Dropbox API</a> in order to list your images.
