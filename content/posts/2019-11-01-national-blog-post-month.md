This is a pretty cool thing, and it turns out my friends in the IndieWeb community may know about it as [there is a wiki page for _National Blog Posting Month_](https://indieweb.org/National_Blog_Posting_Month).

I've decided that I too will be celebrating it, and will be doing it as a way to help me push out posts that are smaller, and have a bit less time invested in them, because some of my posts are incredibly long and require a lot of time to get them written. But I want to improve my ability to write something quickly, so that's what's happening.
