## Commentary

The year I was actually starting to blog about various things I was finding - which [I didn't term blogumentation](https://www.jvt.me/posts/2017/06/25/blogumentation/) until the middle of the year - as well as a few other persuasive things like [_Why You Should Use GitLab.com_](https://www.jvt.me/posts/2017/03/25/why-you-should-use-gitlab/).

There were bits of traffic over the year gaining a little traction, but for the most part, these posts were along my journey playing with Chef at work.

I can't remember what the period of absolutely 0 traffic was for a couple of months, but I assume it was back when I hadn't quite got Nginx and Let's Encrypt playing nicely together.

There's also some interesting commentary in my [2017 in review post](https://www.jvt.me/posts/2018/02/14/2017-in-review/#personal-website).
